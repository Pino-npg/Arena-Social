DEMO (free)
Just pure fun with no bonuses, have fun with your favorite character.
Choose your Nickname and character and click on 1vs1 mode or Tournament.
The game starts when the second player clicks on demo mode.
Initiative dice roll decides who strikes first.
Each player has 80 health points and deals 1D8 damage.
If a player deals 8 damage, the opponent is stunned one turn
and subtracts 1 damage from their next attack.
