DEMO (free)
Just pure fun with no bonuses, have fun with your favorite character.
Choose your character and click on demo mode.
The game start when the second player click on demo mode.

Each player has 30 health points and deals 1D8 damage.
If a player deals 8 damage, the opponent is stunned for one
turn and will subtract 1 damage from his next attack.
