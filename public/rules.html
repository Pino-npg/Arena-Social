Game Overview

Welcome to the battle arena! Choose your character and enter the duel. Each player starts with base HP 20.

Modes

  • Demo: Free mode, no NFTs required. Standard stats.
  • Connect Wallet Free: Use your YUMI NFTs to gain bonuses based on rarity.

Turn Logic

Players take turns rolling dice. Critical hits stun the opponent for -1 damage next turn. Bonus stats from NFTs are applied automatically at battle start.
